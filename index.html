App for manual signatures processing for the xDai bridge

This application allows you to send signatures gathered by the xDai bridge oracles in case if your withdrawal of xDai from the xDai chain is stuck due to high gas fees or for another reason. Enter in the input field the transaction id of the transfer you made on the xDai chain to the bridge contract and press button.




Specify the transaction hash where xDai transfer happened or relayTokens method was called

NOTE: This application is alpha and working on your own risk.
